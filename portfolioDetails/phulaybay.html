PHULAY BAY

COLLATERAL & MAP DESIGN

About the project

The brochure for The Ritz-Carlton Phulay Bay, a luxury resort in Krabi, Thailand, captures the essence of the high-end property through vibrant colors and dramatic images that reflect the natural beauty of the surroundings. Highlighting the resort's villas and private pavilions, the brochure exudes elegance and tranquility. A distinctive feature are the hand-drawn, detailed maps presented in a unique old-world classic style. This artistic touch complements the luxurious feel of the resort, offering a nostalgic journey back to simpler times, perfectly aligning with the serene and opulent atmosphere of Phulay Bay.
